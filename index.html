Welcome to the BrainPicker JavaScript Quiz!

Take a shot at the BrainPicker challenge! The challenge? Why it's a quiz! A quiz about JavaScript. Don't you worry, it's nothing too difficult, just a little something to pick your brain.

The quiz is timed, 15 seconds per question. For each question you get right, you will receive an addtional 5 seconds on the clock. However, for every wrong answer 10 seconds will be deducted from the timer.

Speed and accuracy are the name of the game. Good luck!
